Any revenue generated from EternETH project fees is used to buy EETH tokens from the market and distribute them as dividends to token holders, proportional to each holders percentage of the total supply.

What is EETH?

  • EETH is an ERC-20 token on the Ethereum blockchain with a total supply of 1 billion tokens. It serves as the native token for the EternETH project, an innovator of decentralized applications (dApps) and protocols. EETH is used for governance voting, enabling holders to participate in decision-making processes. It also grants access to dividends, where project revenues are used to buy back EETH tokens and distribute them proportionally to holders.

Features:

  • EternETH revenues used for EETH buys to fund dividends.
  • Enhanced Token Utility: EETH serves as the native dividend currency.
  • Aligned Incentives: EETH dividends tie holder rewards to the token’s success.

How to get EETH tokens:

Step 1:

  • Download Metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Step 2:

  • Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $EETH. If you dont have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Step 3:

  • Connect to UniSwap or EternETH Dashboard. Go to https://app.uniswap.org/swap in your desktop broswer or on the browser inside your Metamask/TrustWallet mobile app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $EETH token address into UniSwap, select EternETH, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Step 4:

  • Switch ETH for $EETH.
