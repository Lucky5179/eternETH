Earn Rewards.

Reflect holders earn automatic rewards in ETH. The rewards are distributed proportionally based on the amount of EternETH held. The more EternETH you hold, the higher your share of the rewards pool.

Features:

  • Rewards are paid automatically in ETH.
  • No need to stake or claim. Rewards add up in your wallet!
  • The more EternETH you hold, the higher percentage of rewards you can receive.
  • Rewards come from fees on trades, so trading volume directly impacts reward payouts.

Tokenomics:

  • Ethereum Rewards Fee: 10%
  • Liquidity Fee: 1%
  • Ecosystem Development Fee: 1%

    • Total tax: 12%

How to get Reflect tokens:

Step 1:

  • Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Step 2:

  • Have BNB in your wallet to switch to $EVERETH. If you do not have any BNB, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Step 3:

  • Connect to PancakeSwap or EternETH Dashboard. Go to pancakeswap.finance/swap in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $EVERETH token address into PancakeSwap, select EternETH, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Step 4:

  • Switch BNB for $EVERETH. We have 12% TAX so you must set a specific slippage of 12-15%.
